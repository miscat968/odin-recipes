Pasta recipe

    Ingredients
  • pasta
  • lots of garlic
  • pepper
  • olive oil
    Steps
  1. Boil pasta
  2. while the pasta is boiling, cut up a few garlic cloves
  3. Cook the garlic cloves in lots of olive oil
  4. Add pasta to the frying pan
  5. add as much salt and pepper till the taste is just right
