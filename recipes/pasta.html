Pasta recipe

    Ingredients
  • pasta
  • lots of garlic
  • pepper
  • olive oil
    Steps
  1. Boil pasta
  2. while the pasta is boiling, cut up a few garlic cloves
  3. Cook the garlic cloves in lots of olive oil
  4. Add pasta to the frying pan
  5. add as much salt and pepper till the taste is just right

This is probably my favourite dish, however, the taste will differ from person to person as some people like large amounts of salt and some people dont.
