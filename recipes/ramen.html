Ramen recipe

Ramen is a delicious Japanese dish that is often considered the Japanese equivalent of fast food by locals

    Ingredients
  • Noodles
  • Spring onions
  • Char siew
  • Your choice of vegetables
    steps
  1. Cut the vegetables and char siew
  2. boil noodles
  3. Prepare the soup
  4. add noodles to soup, then add the char siew and vegetables
